Top 10 Life Hacks

17/01/22

Battery Test

Bounce batteries to see if their good or bad. Drop them on a table from about 6 inches. If they give on small bounce and fall right over, they're good. If they bounce around any more than that, they're dead or on the way out.

Nutella Treat

Once you've almost finished your jar of nutella. For a super chocalatey treat, fill your jar with ice cream and enjoy .

Crayon trick

If you ever need emergency light, us a crayon. Crayons are flammable and will provide about an hour of light as a candle, if you lit the top.

Post It Note Tip

Before you throw away a post-it note, run it between the keys on your keyboard to collect crumbs and fluff.

Iced Cold Beverage on the go

Fill a water bottle a quater of the way full, put it in the freezer and lay the bottle on its side. This way when frozen you can fill with beverage of your choice and have an ice cold drink on the go.

Cool down at home on a hot summers day

Run your wrists under cold water or rub an ice cube on your pulse points to cool down all over quickly, when you get overheated.

Phone Charger Tip

If you put glow in the dark paint on your phone charger, you will never fumble in the dark again.

Want to get your facts straight?

For research use scholar.google.com instead. You'll get search results from trusted sources you can cite when writing papers or developing presentations.

Eye Strain Tip

The 20-20-20 rule (looking at something 20ft away for 20 seconds every 20minutes) is a method proven to stop eye strain and thus stop headaches.

Hunger Advice

Hunger is you bodies way of measuring how soon you should eat, not how much you should eat. If you're super hungry, it just means you need to eat now, not eat a lot.
